#### What have you done for your project so far?

I've been able to create the code that calculates and outputs the punnet squares for genotypes of any size, and the punnet square also color codes identical genotypes for ease of use. It also accounts for Mendlian inheritance and incomplete dominance, and it outputs the probabilities of a progeny having any given genotype. It also produces the punnet squares for both the F1 (children of Parents) and F2 generation (breed of heterozygous F1, which is a common concept in genetics to look at the inheritance of recessive traits and heterozygous F1 genotypes). 

#### What have you not done for your project yet?

I will be finishing the recessive epistasis, dominant epistasis, co-dominance, and hierarchical dominance components of my projects so those punnet squares with those inheritance patterns can be produced. 

#### What problems, if any, have you encountered?

A lot of it has to do with figuring out how to color code the Punnet squares as well as troubleshooting how to iterate through a given input of genotype. Also, incomplete dominance is pretty difficult to work with because the principles of inheritance are not as easily calculable as they are with Mendlian genetics.
